# WakeUp or Else - Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 18, 2026

## Introduction

WakeUp or Else ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

The app operates entirely on your device with no internet connection required. All alarm data is stored locally on your device using Android's Room database.

## Permissions Used

The app requests the following permissions to function properly:

### Required Permissions

1. **SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM** (Android 12+)
   - **Purpose**: Schedule alarms at exact times
   - **Data Access**: None. Only used to set system alarms
   
2. **USE_EXACT_ALARM**
   - **Purpose**: Alternative permission for exact alarm scheduling
   - **Data Access**: None

3. **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED**
   - **Purpose**: Reschedule enabled alarms after device restart
   - **Data Access**: None

4. **FOREGROUND_SERVICE** & **FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PLAYBACK**
   - **Purpose**: Play alarm sound and vibrate when alarm triggers
   - **Data Access**: None

5. **POST_NOTIFICATIONS** (Android 13+)
   - **Purpose**: Display alarm notifications
   - **Data Access**: None

6. **USE_FULL_SCREEN_INTENT**
   - **Purpose**: Show alarm screen over lock screen
   - **Data Access**: None

7. **WAKE_LOCK**
   - **Purpose**: Keep screen on during alarm
   - **Data Access**: None

8. **VIBRATE**
   - **Purpose**: Vibrate phone when alarm rings
   - **Data Access**: None

### Optional Permissions

9. **ACTIVITY_RECOGNITION**
   - **Purpose**: Count steps for the "walk 30 steps" wake-up task
   - **Data Access**: Step count from device sensors
   - **Note**: Step data is only used temporarily during alarm dismissal and is NOT stored or transmitted

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device:
- Alarm times and settings
- Selected wake-up task types
- Repeat schedules

**This data:**
- Never leaves your device
- Is not uploaded to any server
- Is not shared with any third party
- Can be deleted by clearing app data or uninstalling the app

## Third-Party Services

The app does NOT use:

- Any third-party SDKs that collect data

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages.
